Part I, HTMLBook Rises

This part contains two chapters. Chapter 1 demonstrates HTMLBook markup, while Chapter 2 is just a slug.

Chapter 1. HTMLBook Markup

This chapter describes and demonstrates the types of markup that might appear in a chapter. See mappings.asciidoc for more information. HTMLBook borrows much of its semantics from the EPUB 3 specification, as applied via the epub:type attribute. Where EPUB semantics come up short, HTMLBook falls back to DocBook semantics, e.g., class="userinput", as described in Inline Markup.
